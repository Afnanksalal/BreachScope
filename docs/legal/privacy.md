# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This policy explains the main data categories and uses for BreachScope.

## Data Collected

BreachScope may collect account information, authentication records, project and repository metadata, scan records, findings, policy records, integration metadata, API key metadata, audit logs, settings, and security or support communications.

Optional provider keys, such as OpenAI or Firecrawl keys, are customer-supplied and encrypted before storage.

## Purposes

- Provide CLI authentication, dashboard access, project management, and scan ingestion.
- Store findings, triage decisions, policy results, evidence exports, and audit history.
- Run customer-enabled integrations with customer-owned credentials.
- Protect the service through rate limiting, abuse prevention, monitoring, and security investigations.
- Maintain documentation, legal notices, product quality, and operational support.

## Sharing

Data may be shared with infrastructure, authentication, database, analytics, email, hosting, logging, security, and support providers that help operate BreachScope. Customer-enabled integrations may send selected findings or notifications to providers configured by the customer.

## Retention

Account records, scan records, findings, settings, and audit logs are retained while needed to provide the service, meet security needs, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or preserve audit history.

## Rights

Depending on location, users may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, export, object to processing, or withdraw consent where consent is the basis for processing.
